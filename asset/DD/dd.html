About Us

Robot Battle & Electronic based exhibition is organised by Electronics Students' Society, University of Kelaniya to enhance the relationship between our young innovators and the industry. This will be an expansive platform to launch new ideas and skills related to electronics

Digital Dreams was initially commenced in the year of 2013 and in 2023, we are to introduce a tournament of Robot Battle with the intention of making Digital Dreams the most outstanding electronics-based event.
